# Enum Values

## Person Types
- `customer` — Current or prospective customer
- `investor` — Investor or potential investor
- `competitor` — Competitor or industry peer

## Tags
- `pricing` — Pricing models, willingness to pay, cost concerns
- `product` — Features, UX, functionality, bugs, requests
- `gtm` — Go-to-market strategy, sales, distribution, channels
- `competitors` — Competitive landscape, alternatives, switching
- `market` — Industry trends, market size, timing, macro factors

## Aggregate Segment Fields
- `type` — Group by person type (customer/investor/competitor)
- `industry` — Group by company industry
- `company` — Group by company name
